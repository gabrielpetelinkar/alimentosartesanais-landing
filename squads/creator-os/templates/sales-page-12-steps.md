## Step 1: Promessa (Headline)

**Rules:** Strong, clear, believable. No exaggerations. Premise-based.

**Headline:**
> _[Write your main headline here]_

**Subheadline:**
> _[Supporting statement]_

**Formula:** "[Result they want] + [qualifier for who] + [timeframe or method]"

**Bad example:** "Ganhe R$10.000 por mes trabalhando de casa!"
**Good example:** "Aprenda a vender seu produto digital todos os dias, mesmo comecando do zero"

## Step 6: Gancho para Oferta (Hook to Offer)

**Transition copy:**
> _"Tudo isso que eu te mostrei — [recap key points] — esta disponivel em um unico lugar..."_

**Rule:** Bridge naturally from content to commercial offer. No abrupt shift.

## Step 11: Garantia (Guarantee)

**Duration:** ___ days (minimum 7 — Brazilian law CDC)

**Guarantee copy:**
> _"Garantia incondicional de [X] dias. Se voce nao gostar, por qualquer motivo, devolvemos 100% do seu dinheiro. Sem perguntas."_

**Rule:** Make the guarantee PROMINENT. Don't hide it in the footer. It reduces perceived risk dramatically.

## Sales Page Checklist

- [ ] All 12 steps present and complete
- [ ] Big Idea clearly communicated
- [ ] Light Copy principles applied (interesting first, persuasive second)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly layout
- [ ] Page loads in under 3 seconds
- [ ] CTA buttons use benefit-driven text
- [ ] Guarantee is prominent (not hidden)
- [ ] Minimum 5 testimonials included
- [ ] Value stack total > 5x actual price
- [ ] No broken links or missing images
