## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (2 Weeks Before Cart Open)

### Week 1: Seed the Idea
- [ ] Write and schedule "something exciting is coming" teaser email
- [ ] Create 2-3 social media posts (behind-the-scenes of course creation)
- [ ] Design waitlist landing page with email capture
- [ ] Write waitlist email: "Join for early access and founding member pricing"
- [ ] Set up email automation for waitlist confirmations

**Waitlist Goal:** _____ sign-ups (target: 10% of your email list)

### Week 2: Build Anticipation
- [ ] Send waitlist promotion email to full list
- [ ] Share sneak peek of Module 1 content (email or social)
- [ ] Prepare free live training content (30-45 min workshop)
- [ ] Promote the free live training (emails + social)
- [ ] Host the free live training
- [ ] At end of training, announce founding member opportunity with date

**Free Training Topic:** _______________________________________

## Phase 2: Cart Open Preparation

### Sales Page (Simple Beta Version)
- [ ] Headline with transformation promise
- [ ] "Who this is for" section
- [ ] Module-by-module breakdown (what they'll learn)
- [ ] Founding member pricing (show full price crossed out)
- [ ] Your credentials/bio
- [ ] Testimonials (if available) or relevant experience
- [ ] FAQ section (3-5 common questions)
- [ ] Clear CTA button ("Become a Founding Member")
- [ ] Guarantee statement (7+ days)
- [ ] Payment page/checkout set up and tested

### Email Sequence (7 Cart-Open Emails)
- [ ] Day 1 — Cart Opens: Announce to waitlist (early access), then full list
- [ ] Day 2 — Value + Proof: Testimonial or client story
- [ ] Day 3 — FAQ: Address top 3 objections
- [ ] Day 4 — Midweek Nudge: Module breakdown + bonuses
- [ ] Day 5 — Urgency: "Only [X] spots remaining"
- [ ] Day 6 — Social Proof: Additional testimonial or behind-the-scenes
- [ ] Day 7 — Cart Close AM: "Last chance — doors close tonight"
- [ ] Day 7 — Cart Close PM: "Final hours — this is it"

### Social Media Support
- [ ] Cart open announcement post
- [ ] 2-3 FAQ/objection handling posts
- [ ] Countdown stories (days 5-7)
- [ ] Behind-the-scenes of preparation
- [ ] Testimonial or result sharing (if available)

---

## Phase 3: Cart Open (5-7 Days)

### Daily Checklist During Cart Open
- [ ] Send scheduled email for the day
- [ ] Post on social media (support the email message)
- [ ] Respond to all DMs and questions within 4 hours
- [ ] Monitor enrollment numbers
- [ ] Adjust messaging if needed (double down on what resonates)

### Cart Close Day
- [ ] Send AM email: "Last chance — doors close tonight at midnight"
- [ ] Send PM email: "Final hours"
- [ ] Post urgency content on social media
- [ ] Close cart at announced deadline (do NOT extend)
- [ ] Send "enrollment closed" confirmation to non-buyers

### Feedback Survey Questions (Send After Each Module)
1. "What was your biggest takeaway from this module?"
2. "What was confusing or unclear?"
3. "What result did you get from the action item?"

### Student Communication
- [ ] Welcome email sent to all founding members
- [ ] Private group created (Facebook, Slack, or WhatsApp)
- [ ] Module delivery schedule shared with students

## Phase 5: Post-Beta

### Collect Results
- [ ] Request video testimonials from founding members
- [ ] Collect written testimonials (specific results)
- [ ] Document transformation stories (before/after)
- [ ] Screenshot any measurable results

### Improve the Course
- [ ] Review all feedback surveys
- [ ] Identify modules that need improvement
- [ ] Add/remove/restructure lessons based on feedback
- [ ] Record final version of each module

### Plan Next Launch
- [ ] Set full-price launch date
- [ ] Update sales page with new testimonials
- [ ] Refine email sequence based on beta learnings
- [ ] Create webinar for full launch (see webinar-script-template.md)

## What You Need vs What You DON'T

### You NEED:
- [ ] Waitlist landing page (1 hour to create)
- [ ] Simple sales page (2-3 hours)
- [ ] 7-email cart open sequence (2-3 hours)
- [ ] Free live training content (2 hours to prepare)
- [ ] Module 1 ready before first delivery date

### You do NOT need:
- All modules recorded upfront (deliver week by week)
- Professional video production (Zoom + slides works)
- A perfect course platform (Google Classroom or Zoom for beta)
- A massive email list (500+ is enough for beta)
- Beautiful graphics or branding (content > aesthetics for beta)
