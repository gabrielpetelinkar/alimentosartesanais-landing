# 30-Day Stories Content Calendar Template

Use this template to plan your Instagram Stories strategy using the Stories 10x method. Fill in each day with specific content based on the theme.

## Weekly Theme Structure

| Day | Theme | Category | Goal |
|-----|-------|----------|------|
| Monday | Discovery | Discovery | Attract new viewers |
| Tuesday | Engagement | Relationship | Build connection via caixinha |
| Wednesday | Value | Relationship | Teach from your method |
| Thursday | Social Proof | Sales | Share testimonials/results |
| Friday | Sell | Sales | 5-Story sales sequence |
| Saturday | Lifestyle | Relationship | Personal, behind-the-scenes |
| Sunday | Rest/Light | Discovery | Light engagement or rest |
