## Weekly Themes

| Week | Theme | Goal | Lead Magnet Tie-In |
|------|-------|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Problem Awareness | Help audience recognize the problem | Mention lead magnet 1-2x |
| 2 | Solution Education | Teach key concepts from your method | CTA to lead magnet in every piece |
| 3 | Social Proof + Stories | Share results and transformation stories | Lead magnet as "next step" |
| 4 | CTA Push | Drive downloads and grow the list | Direct promotion of lead magnet |
