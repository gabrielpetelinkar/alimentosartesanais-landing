## Action Items

### Phase 1: Define the Success Path
The Success Path is the backbone of your membership:

```
POINT A (Where they start)
    ↓
MILESTONE 1: [First Quick Win]
    ↓
MILESTONE 2: [Core Skill Developed]
    ↓
MILESTONE 3: [Advanced Application]
    ↓
MILESTONE 4: [Mastery/Independence]
    ↓
POINT B (Where they want to be)
```

Each milestone has:
- Clear definition of "done"
- Content/training to reach it
- Community support
- Celebration when achieved

### Phase 2: Design Content Calendar (Curation > Creation)
Monthly content model:

| Week | Content Type | Format |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Week 1 | New training (1 core lesson) | Video + worksheet |
| Week 2 | Live Q&A / Hot seat | Live call (recorded) |
| Week 3 | Guest expert or case study | Interview or presentation |
| Week 4 | Community challenge or workshop | Interactive exercise |

**Key principle: Curation over creation.**
- Don't overwhelm members with too much content
- Guide them to the RIGHT content at the RIGHT time
- Less content, more community
- Monthly "what to focus on" email

### Phase 3: Build Onboarding
First 7 days determine if members stay or leave:

**Day 0:** Welcome email + "Start Here" page
**Day 1:** Quick win exercise (something they can complete in 30 min)
**Day 2:** Introduce the community (where to connect, norms)
**Day 3:** Success Path overview (show them the journey)
**Day 4:** First core content piece
**Day 5:** Encourage first community post
**Day 7:** Check-in email ("How's it going?")

### Phase 4: Retention System
Internal marketing (keep existing members):
- Monthly "progress check" emails
- Celebrate milestones publicly
- Fresh content every month
- Community events and challenges
- Member spotlights and success stories

Churn prevention:
- Exit survey when someone cancels
- "Win-back" email sequence for lapsed members
- Annual pricing option (reduces churn)
- Engagement tracking (flag inactive members)

### Phase 5: Launch the Membership
Options:
1. **Founding Members** (recommended first):
   - Offer to existing customers at special price
   - Limited spots (scarcity)
   - Founding member benefits (locked-in price, extra access)

2. **Open/Close Cart**:
   - Open enrollment windows (2-3x/year)
   - Creates urgency and demand
   - Use PLF structure for each opening

3. **Evergreen**:
   - Always open
   - Lower urgency but consistent revenue
   - Works best after establishing reputation

### Phase 6: Pricing Strategy
Common membership pricing:
- $27-$47/month: Content + community
- $47-$97/month: Content + community + live calls
- $97-$197/month: All above + group coaching
- $197+/month: All above + personalized feedback

Annual option: Offer 2 months free (10-month price for 12 months)

## Veto Conditions
- No existing customers → STOP, need customers first (Phases 3-5)
- No clear ongoing value → STOP, membership needs continuous benefit
- Creator can't commit to monthly content → WARN, consider quarterly membership
- Trying to include everything in membership → STOP, focus on ONE success path
