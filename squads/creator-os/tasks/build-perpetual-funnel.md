## Action Items

### Phase 1: Lead Magnet and Landing Page

**Lead Magnet (Isca Digital):**
- Format: Free class (15-20 min video), ebook, or checklist
- Must deliver real value and showcase your expertise
- Must be directly related to what your paid product teaches
- Must leave the viewer wanting MORE (the course is the "more")

**Landing Page Structure:**
```
Headline: [Main promise/premise of your product niche]
Subheadline: [What they will learn/receive for free]
Bullet points: [3-5 key benefits]
Lead magnet description: [Format + what's inside]
CTA: [Email capture form — "Quero acesso gratis"]
Social proof: [Number of students, short testimonial]
```

**Landing Page Rules:**
- ONE page, ONE objective: capture the lead
- No menu, no distractions, no external links
- Mobile-first design (80%+ traffic is mobile)
- Load time under 3 seconds
- Target conversion rate: 25-35%

### Phase 2: Sales Page (12-Step Architecture)

| Step | Name | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| 1 | Promessa | Strong, clear, believable headline |
| 2 | Para Quem E / Nao E | State who should and shouldn't buy |
| 3 | Prova Social | Testimonials, screenshots, numbers |
| 4 | Historia | Your origin story with scenic details |
| 5 | Conteudo | Product modules and differentials |
| 6 | Gancho para Oferta | Natural transition to the commercial offer |
| 7 | Oferta | Price, payment options, bonuses, value stack |
| 8 | Objecao | Handle the top 4-5 reasons people don't buy |
| 9 | Mais Prova | Additional testimonials, shorter format |
| 10 | CTA com Reforco | Call to action reinforcing the core benefit |
| 11 | Garantia | Minimum 7 days (Brazilian law CDC), recommend 15-30 |
| 12 | Despedida | Final message, light urgency, last CTA |

Target conversion rate: 1-5% (cold traffic), 3-8% (warm traffic)

### Phase 3: Checkout Optimization

- Minimize form fields (name, email, CPF, payment only)
- Show security badges and SSL certificate
- Repeat the guarantee on checkout page
- Add 1-2 testimonials on checkout sidebar
- Offer PIX discount (immediate cash)
- Enable boleto bancario for those without credit card
- **Order bump:** Small complementary product (R$27-R$97) — target 15-30% take rate

### Phase 4: Email Sequences

**Welcome Sequence (5 emails, daily for 5 days):**
1. Deliver lead magnet + introduce yourself + set expectations
2. Share your story (relatability) + one key insight
3. Teach something valuable + soft mention of product
4. Case study / testimonial + direct offer
5. Final push with urgency + guarantee reminder

**Cart Recovery Sequence (3 emails):**
1. (1h after abandonment) "Esqueceu algo? Aqui esta seu link" + FAQ
2. (24h) Testimonial + objection handling + link
3. (48h) Last chance + small bonus or discount

**Perpetual Nurture (ongoing, 2-3 per week):**
- 70% value content, 30% pitch
- Types: story-based, content-based, proof-based, objection-based, urgency-based
- Each email should be standalone

### Phase 5: Upsell/Downsell Flow

| Type | Timing | Price Range | Conversion Target |
|------|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| Order Bump | At checkout | R$27-R$97 | 15-30% |
| Upsell | After purchase | R$297-R$1.997 | 5-15% |
| Downsell | If upsell declined | R$47-R$197 | 10-20% |

### Phase 6: Traffic Strategy

**Paid Traffic (start here for predictability):**
- Platform: Facebook/Instagram Ads
- Initial budget: R$30-50/day
- Campaign: Conversion → Lead capture
- Test 3 ad creatives (A/B/C test)
- CPL target: R$2-R$15 depending on niche

**Organic Traffic (add for margin):**
- Instagram Stories (Stories 10x method)
- Reels for new follower acquisition
- YouTube/Podcast for authority building

### Phase 7: Daily Metrics Dashboard

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| CPL (Custo por Lead) | R$2-R$15 | Fix ads or landing page |
| Landing page conversion | 25-35% | Fix headline, copy, or design |
| Sales page conversion | 1-5% | Fix sales page (12 steps) |
| Checkout conversion | 30-50% | Fix checkout experience |
| Email open rate | 15-25% | Fix subject lines |
| Email click rate | 2-5% | Fix email copy and CTAs |
| ROAS | 2x minimum, target 3-5x | Optimize weakest funnel stage |
| Ticket medio | > product price | Improve order bump/upsell take rates |
