3. "What social media platforms do you use? How engaged is your audience?"
4. "Have you ever sold anything to your audience before?"

### Step 2: Beta Strategy
1. "How many founding members do you want? (Recommended: 10-30)"
2. "What's your future full price going to be?"
3. "What discount feels right for founding members? (Recommended: 30-40% off)"
4. "Are you comfortable teaching the course live week by week?"

### Step 3: Timeline
1. "When do you want to open cart? (Recommend 2-3 weeks from now)"
2. "How long will your cart be open? (Recommended: 5-7 days)"
3. "Can you commit to delivering one module per week after cart close?"

## Action Items

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (2 Weeks Before Cart Open)

**Week 1: Seed the Idea**
- Day 1-3: Email your list with a story related to your topic. End with: "Something exciting is coming. Stay tuned."
- Day 3-5: Social posts — behind-the-scenes of you creating the course
- Day 5-7: Build a waitlist landing page (simple: headline + email capture)

**Week 2: Build Anticipation**
- Day 8-10: Send waitlist email: "Join the waitlist for early access and founding member pricing"
- Day 10-12: Share a sneak peek of Module 1 content (email or social)
- Day 12-14: Host a free live training (30-45 min) that teaches one key concept from the course. End with: "The full system opens for founding members on [date]."

**Waitlist Goal:** 10% of your email list (e.g., 2,000 subscribers → 200 waitlist sign-ups)

### Phase 2: Cart Open (5-7 Days)

**Email Sequence:**

| Day | Email | Focus |
|-----|-------|-------|
| Day 1 | Cart Opens | Announce to waitlist first (early access), then full list |
| Day 2 | Value + Proof | Share a testimonial or client story related to the transformation |
| Day 3 | FAQ | Address top 3 objections ("Is this for me?" "Do I have time?" "Will it work?") |
| Day 4 | Midweek Nudge | Share what's included — module breakdown + bonuses |
| Day 5 | Urgency | "Only [X] founding member spots remaining" |
| Day 6 | Social Proof | Share additional testimonials or behind-the-scenes |
| Day 7 | Cart Close | AM: "Last chance — doors close tonight." PM: "Final hours." |

**Sales Page (Simple Beta Version):**
- Headline with transformation promise
- Who this is for
- Module-by-module breakdown
- Founding member pricing (show full price crossed out)
- Testimonials (if available) or your credentials
- FAQ section
- Clear CTA button

### Phase 3: Deliver the Course Live

**Weekly Delivery Schedule:**
- Week 1 after cart close: Deliver Module 1 (live via Zoom or pre-recorded + live Q&A)
- Week 2: Module 2 + collect feedback survey
- Continue weekly through all modules
- Final week: Graduation + testimonial collection

**Feedback Loop (After Each Module):**
Send a 3-question survey:
1. "What was your biggest takeaway from this module?"
2. "What was confusing or unclear?"
3. "What result did you get from the action item?"

Use this feedback to improve the course before your next launch.

### Phase 4: Post-Beta

1. Collect video testimonials from founding members
2. Refine course content based on feedback
3. Record the final version of each module
4. Plan your next launch (full price) or pass to @russell-brunson for offer design

## Veto Conditions
- No course outline at all → WARN, at least create a rough draft of modules before beta
- Email list under 100 → WARN, may need to combine with social media and personal outreach
- Creator wants to build the full course before selling → STOP, redirect to beta-first approach
- No feedback collection plan → STOP, add feedback surveys (the whole point of beta is learning)
- Cart open longer than 10 days → WARN, urgency diminishes after 7 days
