## Action Items

### Phase 1: Build Perpetual Funnel (VTSD)
The perpetual funnel runs 24/7, not just during launches:

```
TRAFFIC (Reels, Posts, Ads, Content)
    ↓
LANDING PAGE (capture email with lead magnet)
    ↓
EMAIL SEQUENCE (nurture + sell over 7-14 days)
    ↓
SALES PAGE (present the offer)
    ↓
CHECKOUT (with order bump)
    ↓
UPSELL PAGE (complementary product)
    ↓
THANK YOU + ONBOARDING
```

### Phase 2: Email Sequence for Perpetual
Unlike launch emails, perpetual emails run continuously:

**Day 1:** Welcome + deliver lead magnet + personal story
**Day 2:** Your biggest insight about [domain] (value)
**Day 3:** Common mistake people make (agitate problem)
**Day 4:** Your framework/method (introduce solution)
**Day 5:** Case study or testimonial (proof)
**Day 6:** FAQ / objection handling
**Day 7:** The offer (with urgency — limited bonus or price increase)

After Day 7: Move to weekly value emails with soft CTAs

### Phase 3: Stories 10x Methodology
The principle: **Reels attract, Stories sell.**

**Daily Stories Framework:**
- **Morning (2-3 stories):** Day in the life, behind the scenes
- **Midday (3-4 stories):** Teaching moment, tip, insight (value)
- **Afternoon (2-3 stories):** Social proof, testimonial, results
- **Evening (2-3 stories):** CTA, offer mention, link to sales page

**Story Types for Conversion:**
1. **Enquete (Poll):** "Voce tem dificuldade com X?" → engagement
2. **Caixa de perguntas:** "Me pergunta sobre X" → conversation
3. **Prova social:** Student results, testimonials
4. **Bastidores:** Behind the scenes of creating content
5. **CTA direto:** "Link na bio" or swipe up to sales page

**The Conversation Formula:**
Stories → Engagement (polls, questions) → DM conversations → Sales

### Phase 4: Metrics Dashboard
Track daily:
- Traffic to landing page
- Email opt-in rate (target: 30-50%)
- Email open rate (target: 20-30%)
- Click rate to sales page (target: 3-5%)
- Sales page conversion (target: 1-3%)
- Daily revenue
- Customer acquisition cost (if using ads)

### Phase 5: Optimization Loop
Weekly:
1. Review metrics dashboard
2. Identify biggest drop-off point in funnel
3. Optimize that ONE point
4. Test for 1 week
5. Repeat

## Veto Conditions
- No product to sell → STOP, go back to Phase 1
- No audience at all (0 followers, 0 email list) → STOP, go back to Phase 4
- Trying to run perpetual AND launches simultaneously without strategy → WARN, choose one focus
- No landing page or sales page → STOP, build the funnel first
