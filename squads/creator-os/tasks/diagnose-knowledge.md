## Elicitation (Required)

### Step 1: Domain Discovery
Ask the creator:
1. "What is your area of expertise? What do you know deeply?"
2. "Who do you help? What transformation do you provide?"
3. "How did you learn this? (years of experience, formal training, self-taught)"
4. "What questions do people ask you most often?"

### Step 2: Knowledge Audit
Ask the creator:
1. "Where is your knowledge stored? (notebooks, apps, your head, files)"
2. "Do you have existing content? (blog posts, videos, social media, presentations)"
3. "What tools do you use for notes? (Notion, Google Docs, Apple Notes, none)"
4. "How organized is your knowledge on a scale of 1-10?"

### Step 3: Goal Alignment
Ask the creator:
1. "What do you want to create? (course, ebook, mentoring program, all of the above)"
2. "Who is your ideal student? What do they struggle with?"
3. "What transformation will your product deliver? (Point A → Point B)"

## Action Items

### Phase 1: Brain Dump (20 min)
1. Set a timer for 20 minutes
2. Write down EVERYTHING you know about your domain
3. One idea per note/bullet
4. Don't edit, don't organize — just dump
5. Include: concepts, processes, tips, stories, mistakes, tools, resources

### Phase 2: PARA Classification (30 min)
For each item from the brain dump, classify into:

**PROJECTS** (active, with deadline):
- What courses/products are you building RIGHT NOW?
- What content are you creating this month?

**AREAS** (ongoing, no deadline):
- What are your ongoing professional responsibilities?
- What domains must you stay current in?

**RESOURCES** (reference, useful someday):
- What topics interest you but aren't active projects?
- What reference material might you need later?

**ARCHIVES** (inactive):
- What past projects or interests are no longer active?

### Phase 3: Topic Identification (20 min)
From the organized knowledge, identify the top 5-10 teachable topics:

### Phase 4: Intermediate Packets (30 min)
For the top 3 topics, create Intermediate Packets:

### Phase 5: Gap Analysis (10 min)
Identify what's missing:
- What topics do students need but you haven't documented?
- What knowledge is in your head but not written down?
- What needs more research or updated information?
