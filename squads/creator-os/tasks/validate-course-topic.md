## Elicitation (Required)

### Step 1: The 10% Edge Check
1. "What topic are you considering for your digital course?"
2. "Have you gotten results in this area — for yourself or for someone else?"
3. "Who would you teach? Describe your ideal student."
4. "Where is your ideal student right now (Point A) and where will they be after your course (Point B)?"
5. "Are you passionate enough about this topic to talk about it for years?"

### Step 2: Audience Research Assessment
1. "Do you have an existing audience? (Email list, social media, community)"
2. "Does your audience already ask you questions about this topic?"
3. "Have you looked at what competitors are offering in this space?"
4. "Are there forums, groups, or communities where people discuss this problem?"

### Step 3: Validation Path
1. "Are you open to doing 5-10 free Course Calls with potential students?"
2. "Would you consider pre-selling or beta launching before building the full course?"
3. "What's your timeline for validating this topic?"

## Action Items

### Phase 1: The 10% Edge Scorecard
Score each question YES or NO:

| # | Question | Answer | Score |
|---|----------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Have I gotten results in this area (for myself or others)? | | |
| 2 | Am I at least 10% ahead of the people I want to teach? | | |
| 3 | Can I articulate a clear Point A → Point B transformation? | | |
| 4 | Do I have stories and examples from my own experience? | | |
| 5 | Am I passionate enough to talk about this for years? | | |

- **5/5 or 4/5**: GREEN LIGHT — proceed to audience research
- **3/5**: YELLOW LIGHT — identify gaps and address before proceeding
- **2/5 or below**: RED LIGHT — consider a different topic

### Phase 2: Audience Research
1. **Survey your audience** (if you have one):
   - Send a 1-question survey: "What's your #1 challenge with [topic]?"
   - Provide 4-5 options plus an open-ended field
   - If 30%+ mention the same problem, you have a validated angle

2. **Research the market**:
   - Search for competing courses (competition = demand)
   - Check online forums, Facebook groups, Reddit for pain points
   - Look at Amazon book reviews in the topic for common struggles
   - Analyze what competitors are NOT doing well

3. **Social listening**:
   - Review DMs, comments, and questions people already ask you
   - Monitor relevant hashtags and conversations

### Phase 3: Course Calls
1. Offer 5-10 free 30-minute calls to people in your audience
2. Ask these key questions:
   - "What are you struggling with most in [topic area]?"
   - "What have you already tried? Why didn't it work?"
   - "If I created a course on [topic], would you be interested?"
   - "What would make this a must-buy for you?"
3. Listen for patterns — struggles mentioned 3+ times become modules

### Phase 4: Pre-Sell Test (Optional but Recommended)
1. Create a simple sales page describing the transformation
2. Offer founding member pricing (20-40% off future price)
3. Set a 7-10 day enrollment window
4. If 10+ people buy → validated, proceed to build
5. If fewer than 10 → refine positioning and try again

## Veto Conditions
- 10% Edge score below 3/5 → STOP, suggest exploring different topics
- No identifiable audience or community for the topic → WARN, recommend list building first
- Topic is too broad (covers everything) → STOP, help narrow to a specific transformation
- Creator cannot articulate Point A → Point B → STOP, work on transformation clarity first
- Zero demand signals after research → STOP, pivot to a different angle or topic
