## Action Items

### Phase 1: Understand the Stories 10x Framework

**Core principle:** Reels atraem seguidores novos. Stories vendem para quem ja te segue.

**The 3 Device Categories:**

| Category | Purpose | Frequency |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| Discovery | Attract new viewers, expand reach | 2x/week |
| Relationship | Build trust, connection, parasocial bond | 3-4x/week |
| Sales | Convert engaged followers into buyers | 2-3x/week |

### Phase 2: Weekly Content Calendar

| Day | Theme | Device Category | Content Focus |
|-----|-------|----------------|---------------|
| Monday | Discovery | Discovery | Hot take or tutorial to attract new viewers |
| Tuesday | Engagement | Relationship | Caixinha de perguntas about a pain point |
| Wednesday | Value | Relationship | Teach something from your method/product |
| Thursday | Social Proof | Sales | Share testimonials and client results |
| Friday | Sell | Sales | Direct sales push with 5-Story sequence |
| Saturday | Lifestyle | Relationship | Personal content, relatability, behind-the-scenes |
| Sunday | Rest/Light | Discovery | Light enquete, behind-the-scenes (optional) |

### Phase 3: Daily Story Sequence

| Time | Type | Stories | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|---------|
| Morning (8-10am) | Connection | 2-4 | Rotina, bastidores, lifestyle |
| Midday (12-2pm) | Value | 3-5 | Teaching, insight, hot take |
| Afternoon (4-6pm) | Engagement | 2-3 | Enquete, caixinha, quiz |
| Evening (7-9pm) | Conversion | 2-4 | Prova social, oferta, CTA |

**Total:** 10-15 Stories per day, spread throughout the day.

### Phase 4: The 5-Story Sales Sequence

Use this framework every Friday (or 2-3x/week):

```
Story 1: HOOK — Provocative question or bold statement about the problem
  "Voce sabia que a maioria das pessoas [your niche] erra nisso?"

Story 2: AGITATION — Show the consequences of NOT solving the problem
  "E por causa disso que [negative consequence]. Eu ja vi isso acontecer centenas de vezes."

Story 3: STORY — Share a case study or your own experience
  "[Client name] estava exatamente nessa situacao. Em [timeframe], ela conseguiu [result]."

Story 4: SOLUTION — Reveal that your product solves this
  "Isso e exatamente o que eu ensino no [product name]. O metodo completo, passo a passo."

Story 5: CTA — Direct link with clear instruction
  "Se voce quer [transformation], clica no link. Vagas abertas."
  [Link sticker to sales page]
```

### Phase 5: DM-to-Sale Pipeline

**Trigger:** Viewer responds to caixinha, enquete, or sends a DM

**Pipeline Steps:**
1. **Acknowledge**: "Boa pergunta! Me conta mais sobre sua situacao..."
2. **Qualify**: "Voce ja tem [relevant context]? Ha quanto tempo?"
3. **Identify pain**: "Qual o maior desafio que voce enfrenta com [topic]?"
4. **Connect**: "Isso e exatamente o que eu ensino no [product name]..."
5. **Handle objection** (if raised): Address specifically
6. **Close**: "Quer que eu te mande o link com todos os detalhes?"

**Rules:**
- Respond to EVERY DM — each one is a potential sale
- Ask questions, don't pitch immediately
- The conversation leads the client to WANT to buy
- You are diagnosing their problem and prescribing the solution

### Phase 6: Key Devices to Use

**Discovery Devices:**
- Caixinha de Perguntas Estrategica (reveal audience pain points)
- Enquete Polarizadora (force strong opinions, no neutral answers)
- Hot take / Opiniao forte (share controversial truths about your niche)
- Repost de Reels nos Stories (bridge Reels viewers into Stories)

**Relationship Devices:**
- Rotina real (show actual daily routine, not curated)
- Bastidores do negocio (behind-the-scenes of your work)
- Vulnerabilidade estrategica (share a struggle with a lesson)
- Tutorial rapido (teach something in 3-5 Stories)
- Storytelling sequencial (multi-Story narrative arc)

**Sales Devices:**
- Prova social em tempo real (share results as they happen)
- Caixinha de objecoes ("O que te impede de comprar?" — answer publicly)
- Antes e depois (transformations from your product)
- Oferta relampago (time-limited offer for Stories viewers)
- Depoimento em video (repost client video testimonials)
