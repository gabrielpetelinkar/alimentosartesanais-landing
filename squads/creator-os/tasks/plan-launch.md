## Action Items

### Phase 1: Choose Launch Type

**Seed Launch** (list < 100):
- Simplest launch — sell to a small group first
- Deliver live, collect testimonials
- Use feedback to improve before bigger launch

**Internal Launch** (list 100-5000):
- Full PLF sequence to your own list
- 3 pieces of pre-launch content
- Open cart for 5-7 days

**JV Launch** (list 5000+):
- Partner with affiliates who promote to their lists
- Larger scale, same PLF structure
- Dream 100 affiliate outreach

### Phase 2: Pre-Launch Content Sequence

**PLC 1 — "The Opportunity"** (Day 1-3):
- Show the opportunity/transformation
- Answer: "Why should I pay attention?"
- Deliver value upfront (teach something useful)
- End with: "In the next piece, I'll show you HOW..."

**PLC 2 — "The Transformation"** (Day 4-6):
- Show the transformation in action
- Answer: "How does this actually work?"
- Case studies, examples, proof
- End with: "In the final piece, I'll show you the complete roadmap..."

**PLC 3 — "The Ownership"** (Day 7-9):
- Help them see themselves doing it
- Answer: "Can I do this too?"
- Address objections directly
- Transition to the offer

### Phase 3: Email Sequences

**Pre-Launch Emails** (before PLC):
- Email 1: "Something big is coming" (anticipation)
- Email 2: "PLC 1 is live" (link to content)
- Email 3: "PLC 2 is live" (link + recap)
- Email 4: "PLC 3 is live" (link + build to cart open)

**Open Cart Emails** (5-7 day window):
- Email 1: "Doors are open" (full offer reveal)
- Email 2: "Success story" (social proof)
- Email 3: "FAQ / Objection handling"
- Email 4: "Behind the scenes" (personal story)
- Email 5: "Last 48 hours" (urgency)
- Email 6: "Last 24 hours" (scarcity)
- Email 7: "Doors closing tonight" (final call)

**Post-Launch Emails**:
- Email 1: "Thank you" (to buyers)
- Email 2: "What's next" (non-buyers, next opportunity)

### Phase 4: Mental Triggers
Apply these throughout the launch:
- **Scarcity**: Limited time, limited spots, price increase
- **Social Proof**: Testimonials, student count, results
- **Authority**: Your credentials, media mentions, endorsements
- **Reciprocity**: Give massive value in PLCs before asking for sale
- **Anticipation**: Tease what's coming, create excitement
- **Community**: Show others are joining, belong to a group
- **Events/Rituals**: Make the launch feel like an EVENT, not a sale

### Phase 5: Post-Launch Debrief
After the cart closes:
1. Total revenue generated
2. Conversion rate (sales / list size)
3. Which PLC performed best
4. Which emails had highest open/click rates
5. What objections weren't addressed
6. What to improve for next launch
