## Action Items

### Phase 1: Design Your Signature Lead Magnet

Choose the format that best fits your audience:

| Type | Description | Best For | Creation Time |
|------|-------------|----------|---------------|
| Cheat Sheet / Checklist (PDF) | Quick-reference document | Most niches | 2-3 hours |
| Video Training | Short video teaching one skill | Visual learners | 3-5 hours |
| Quiz / Assessment | Interactive tool with personalized results | Engagement | 5-8 hours |
| Template / Swipe File | Done-for-you resource | Action-oriented audiences | 2-4 hours |
| Mini Course / Email Challenge | Multi-day free training | High perceived value | 8-12 hours |
| Webinar Registration | Free live training | Launch preparation | 3-5 hours |

**Lead Magnet Rules:**
- Solves ONE specific problem related to your course topic
- Delivers a quick win (result within 30 minutes of consuming it)
- Attracts people who would be interested in your paid course
- Makes them think: "If the free stuff is this good, the paid course must be amazing"

### Phase 2: Build the 30-Day Content Calendar

**Weekly Theme Structure:**

| Week | Theme | Goal |
|------|-------|------|
| Week 1 | Problem Awareness | Help audience recognize they have the problem your course solves |
| Week 2 | Solution Education | Teach key concepts from your methodology (give real value) |
| Week 3 | Social Proof + Stories | Share results, testimonials, and your own transformation story |
| Week 4 | CTA + Lead Magnet Push | Drive downloads of your lead magnet and grow your list |

**Daily Content Types (Choose 3-5 per week):**
- **Teaching posts**: Tips, how-tos, frameworks from your expertise
- **Story posts**: Personal stories, client transformations, lessons learned
- **Myth-busting**: Debunk common misconceptions in your niche
- **Behind-the-scenes**: Show your process, your work, your life
- **Engagement posts**: Questions, polls, challenges for your audience
- **CTA posts**: Direct invitation to download your lead magnet

### Phase 3: Welcome Email Sequence

| Email | Timing | Content |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Email 1 | Immediately | Deliver the lead magnet + introduce yourself briefly |
| Email 2 | Day 2 | Share your story — why you do what you do |
| Email 3 | Day 3-4 | Provide additional value related to the lead magnet topic |
| Email 4 | Day 5-7 | Share a success story or testimonial |
| Email 5 | Day 7-10 | Invite to the next step (webinar, waitlist, or offer) |

### Phase 4: Distribution Plan

**7 Places to Share Your Lead Magnet:**
1. Dedicated landing page on your website
2. Social media bio links (Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok)
3. Within your content (podcast episodes, blog posts, YouTube descriptions)
4. Guest appearances (podcasts, summits, collaborations)
5. Facebook/Instagram ads (when ready for paid traffic)
6. Pinterest pins (long-term organic traffic)
7. Email signature (passive, always-on promotion)

### Phase 5: Set Metrics and Targets

| Metric | Target | How to Track |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Lead magnet landing page conversion | 30-50% | Email platform analytics |
| Email open rate | 20-30% | Email platform |
| Email click rate | 2-5% | Email platform |
| List growth rate | 100-500 new subscribers/month | Weekly tracking |
| Content engagement rate | 3-5% | Platform analytics |
| Webinar registration rate from email | 25-40% | Registration page analytics |

## Veto Conditions
- No clear connection between lead magnet and paid course → STOP, realign the lead magnet
- Content plan with no CTA posts → STOP, at least 20% of content should drive list growth
- No email welcome sequence → STOP, new subscribers need to be nurtured immediately
- Trying to be on every platform at once → WARN, master one platform first, then expand
- Lead magnet tries to solve too many problems → STOP, focus on ONE quick win
