### Phase 2: CTA Strategy (Graduated)

**The 80/15/5 Rule:**
- 80% of content = Pure value (soft CTA: follow, save, share)
- 15% of content = Value + product mention (medium CTA: newsletter, free resource)
- 5% of content = Direct promotion (hard CTA: buy, apply, book call)

**CTA Types & Templates:**

```
SOFT CTAs (80% of content)
- "Follow for more [topic] insights"
- "Save this for later"
- "Share this with someone who needs it"
- "What's your take? Comment below"
- "Double tap if this resonates"

MEDIUM CTAs (15% of content)
- "I go deeper on this in my newsletter → [link in bio]"
- "I created a free [resource] on this topic → [link]"
- "Want the full framework? DM me '[keyword]'"
- "Every week I break this down in my newsletter. 50K+ readers."
- "I just published a deep-dive on this → [link]"

HARD CTAs (5% of content)
- "I built [product] to solve exactly this. Here's what's inside → [link]"
- "Enrollment for [program] closes [date]. [Link]"
- "If you want help with this, I have [X] spots for [service]. Apply → [link]"
- "This is what we teach inside [product]. Join [X] others → [link]"
```

**CTA Placement by Platform:**

| Platform | Soft CTA | Medium CTA | Hard CTA |
|----------|----------|------------|----------|
| LinkedIn | End of post | Mention product naturally in body | Dedicated promotional post |
| X/Twitter | Last tweet in thread | Bio link mention | Pinned tweet + dedicated post |
| Instagram Feed | Caption end | Link in bio reference | Story swipe-up / post with link |
| Instagram Stories | Poll/question | Swipe up to free resource | Direct product promo |
| YouTube | End screen subscribe | Video description link | Dedicated sponsor/product segment |
| Newsletter | PS line | Natural mention in content | Dedicated section or full email |
