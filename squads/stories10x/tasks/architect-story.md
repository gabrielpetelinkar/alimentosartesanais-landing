## Action Items

### Phase 1: Structure Each Story with SB7

For each selected story (3-5), apply the SB7 narrative arc:

```
STRUCTURED STORY: [Title]

1. HOOK (first 1-2 sentences)
   - Pattern interrupt that stops the scroll/grabs attention
   - Creates curiosity gap or emotional tension
   - Example: "I was 24 hours away from shutting down my business."

2. CHARACTER (the hero — your audience, or you as proxy)
   - Who are they?
   - What do they want?
   - What's at stake?

3. PROBLEM
   - External: The tangible challenge
   - Internal: How it makes them feel (frustrated, overwhelmed, stuck)
   - Philosophical: Why it shouldn't be this way

4. GUIDE (you enter the story)
   - Empathy: "I've been there. I know what that feels like."
   - Authority: "Here's why I can help — [credential/result]."

5. PLAN (the path forward)
   - Step 1: [First clear action]
   - Step 2: [Next step]
   - Step 3: [Final step]

6. SUCCESS (the transformation)
   - Paint the vivid picture of life AFTER
   - Specific results, emotions, changes

7. FAILURE (the stakes)
   - What happens if they DON'T act?
   - Make the cost of inaction clear

8. CALL TO ACTION
   - Direct CTA or transitional CTA depending on context
```

### Phase 2: Identify the Pillar Story

The Pillar Story is the ONE story that:
- Best encapsulates your brand's mission
- Creates the strongest emotional connection
- Can be told in 30 seconds or 30 minutes (scalable)
- Works across all platforms and contexts
- Makes your audience think "this person GETS me"

**Pillar Story Selection Criteria:**
| Criterion | Weight | Score (1-5) |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Emotional resonance | 25% | |
| Brand alignment (SB7) | 25% | |
| Scalability (works at any length) | 20% | |
| Uniqueness (only YOU can tell this) | 15% | |
| Call-to-action potential | 15% | |

**Pillar Story Structure (Extended):**
```
PILLAR STORY: [Title]

SETUP (The Old World)
- Where were you before? What was life like?
- What was your audience's world like?

CONFLICT (The Problem)
- What happened? What broke?
- What struggle did you/they face?

GUIDE MOMENT (The Turning Point)
- How did you discover the solution/framework?
- What was the "aha" moment?

RESOLUTION (The Transformation)
- What changed? What results did you/they get?
- What does life look like now?

MORAL (The Lesson for the Audience)
- What should THEY take away?
- How does this connect to YOUR offer/mission?
```

### Phase 3: Create Story Variations

For each structured story, create variations:

**Social Media Version (50-150 words)**
- Hook + compressed story + single takeaway + CTA
- Optimized for scroll-stopping

**Email Version (300-500 words)**
- Full narrative arc with personal tone
- Cliffhanger or lesson at the end
- Clear CTA

**Long-Form Version (800-1500 words)**
- Complete SB7 structure with all details
- For blog posts, newsletter deep-dives, webinar scripts
- Multiple CTAs woven naturally

**Video Script Version (1-3 min)**
- Visual cues included
- Pacing notes (slow down here, speed up there)
- B-roll suggestions

### Phase 4: Story-to-Content Mapping

Create a map showing which story serves which purpose:

| Story | Best For | Primary Platform | CTA Type | Frequency |
|-------|----------|-----------------|----------|-----------|
| Pillar Story | Brand building, about pages | All | Transitional | Monthly |
| Origin Story | Trust building, introductions | Email, LinkedIn | Soft | Bi-weekly |
| Client Success | Social proof, sales | Instagram, Sales page | Direct | Weekly |
| Lesson Story | Value + authority | X, LinkedIn, Newsletter | Medium | Weekly |
| Struggle Story | Vulnerability, connection | Instagram Stories, Email | Soft | Monthly |
