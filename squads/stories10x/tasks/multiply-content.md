## Action Items

### Phase 1: Build the Content Pyramid

For each structured story, create the pyramid:

```
CONTENT PYRAMID: [Story Title]

PILLAR CONTENT (1 piece — the source)
├── Long-form video (10-20 min) or blog post (1500+ words) or podcast episode
│
├── MEDIUM PIECES (5-8 pieces — chopped from pillar)
│   ├── LinkedIn article (600-800 words)
│   ├── Newsletter deep-dive (800-1200 words)
│   ├── YouTube video (5-8 min, key segment)
│   ├── Instagram carousel (8-10 slides)
│   ├── X/Twitter thread (8-12 tweets)
│   └── Audio clip for podcast/stories (2-3 min)
│
└── MICRO PIECES (15-30 pieces — atomic content)
    ├── Instagram Reel (30-60 sec, key moment)
    ├── TikTok (15-60 sec, hook + payoff)
    ├── YouTube Short (30-60 sec)
    ├── X/Twitter single post (key quote/insight)
    ├── LinkedIn post (key lesson + CTA)
    ├── Instagram Story sequence (5-7 slides)
    ├── Quote graphic (key phrase on branded template)
    ├── Behind-the-scenes photo/video
    ├── Poll/question derived from the topic
    └── Meme/relatable format based on the lesson
```

### Phase 2: Platform-Native Adaptation

**Each platform gets its OWN version — never cross-post the same content.**

**Instagram:**
- Reels: 30-60 sec, vertical, hook in first 1 sec, text overlay, trending audio optional
- Carousel: 8-10 slides, first slide is the hook, last slide is CTA
- Stories: Behind-the-scenes, polls, questions, casual tone
- Feed post: Strong visual + micro-story in caption (2200 char max)

**LinkedIn:**
- Post: Professional tone, line breaks for readability, hook in first 2 lines (before "see more"), 1300 char sweet spot
- Article: Longer thought pieces, more analytical, case studies welcome
- Tone: Insights > entertainment, lessons > stories

**X/Twitter:**
- Single tweet: One powerful insight, 280 char, no hashtags
- Thread: 8-12 tweets, each standalone valuable, numbered, first tweet is THE hook
- Tone: Punchy, opinionated, conversational

**YouTube:**
- Long-form: 10-20 min, strong title + thumbnail, retention-focused editing
- Shorts: 30-60 sec, vertical, hook in first 2 sec, loop-worthy
- Tone: Educational, personality-driven

**TikTok:**
- 15-60 sec, vertical, fast pace, hook in 1 sec
- Trending sounds when relevant
- Native feel (not polished — authentic)

**Newsletter/Email:**
- Personal tone, story-driven, one core message
- CTA at the end, soft or direct depending on content
- 800-1500 words

**Rules:**
- Maximum 1 Right Hook per 4-5 Jabs
- Never post the same format 2 days in a row
- Pillar story appears 3-5x across the 2 weeks (different angles)
- Each platform gets platform-native content (not cross-posts)
- Weekend content can be lighter/behind-the-scenes

### Phase 5: Document, Don't Create — Capture System

Set up the ongoing capture system:
- Phone notes app: Story ideas, observations, quotes
- Voice memos: Capture stories as they happen
- Screenshot folder: Content inspiration, data, testimonials
- "Content capture" daily habit: 5 minutes noting today's stories
