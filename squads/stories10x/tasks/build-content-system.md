### Phase 1: Define Content Pillars & Templates

**Content Pillars (3-5 topics):**
```
PILLAR 1: [Topic] — [Why this matters to your audience]
  Templates: Observation, How-I, Framework
  Frequency: 2x/week

PILLAR 2: [Topic] — [Why this matters]
  Templates: Story→Lesson, Contrarian, Mistake
  Frequency: 2x/week

PILLAR 3: [Topic] — [Why this matters]
  Templates: Listicle, Before/After, Question
  Frequency: 1x/week

PILLAR 4: [Personal/Behind-the-scenes]
  Templates: Story→Lesson, Unpopular Opinion
  Frequency: 1x/week

PILLAR 5: [Promotional — product/offer related]
  Templates: How-I, Client Success, Before/After
  Frequency: 1x/week (max)
```

**10 Content Templates:**

```
TEMPLATE 1: OBSERVATION
Hook: "I noticed [observation about industry/audience]"
Body: Expand on the observation + why it matters + your take
CTA: "What's your experience with this?"
Best for: LinkedIn, X | Pillar: Any | Jab

TEMPLATE 2: LISTICLE
Hook: "[N] things about [topic] that [audience] should know"
Body: Numbered list with brief explanations
CTA: "Save this for later" or "Which resonates most?"
Best for: LinkedIn, Instagram carousel | Pillar: Any | Jab

TEMPLATE 3: STORY → LESSON
Hook: "[Compelling opening line of story]"
Body: Brief story (3-5 sentences) → Clear lesson
CTA: "The takeaway: [one sentence]"
Best for: All platforms | Pillar: Any | Jab

TEMPLATE 4: CONTRARIAN
Hook: "Most people believe [common belief]. I disagree."
Body: Why the common belief is wrong + your alternative view + evidence
CTA: "Agree or disagree? Let me know below"
Best for: LinkedIn, X | Pillar: Any | Jab

TEMPLATE 5: HOW I...
Hook: "How I [achieved result] in [timeframe]"
Body: Step-by-step process (3-7 steps)
CTA: "Want the full breakdown? [link/follow]"
Best for: All platforms | Pillar: Any | Jab/Medium CTA

TEMPLATE 6: MISTAKE
Hook: "The biggest mistake I see in [area]"
Body: Describe the mistake + why people make it + what to do instead
CTA: "Have you made this mistake?"
Best for: LinkedIn, X, Reels | Pillar: Any | Jab

TEMPLATE 7: FRAMEWORK
Hook: "My [N]-step framework for [outcome]"
Body: Framework steps with brief explanations
CTA: "I go deeper on this in [product/newsletter]"
Best for: LinkedIn carousel, X thread | Pillar: Any | Medium CTA

TEMPLATE 8: BEFORE/AFTER
Hook: "[Time] ago I was [before]. Today I'm [after]."
Body: The contrast + what changed + how
CTA: "Here's what I learned" or "DM me to learn how"
Best for: Instagram, LinkedIn | Pillar: Personal | Jab

TEMPLATE 9: UNPOPULAR OPINION
Hook: "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]"
Body: Reasoning + evidence + why this matters
CTA: "Thoughts?"
Best for: X, LinkedIn | Pillar: Any | Jab

TEMPLATE 10: CLIENT SUCCESS
Hook: "[Client name/type] went from [before] to [after]"
Body: The challenge + the process + the result
CTA: "Want similar results? [CTA]"
Best for: Instagram, LinkedIn | Pillar: Promotional | Right Hook
```

### Phase 2: Weekly Content Sprint

```
WEEKLY CONTENT SPRINT

BATCH DAY (Creator's chosen day — ~2-3 hours)
├── 30 min: IDEA SELECTION
│   ├── Review Idea Bank (captured throughout the week)
│   ├── Select 5-7 ideas for the week
│   ├── Match each idea to a template
│   └── Assign to content pillar
│
├── 90 min: BATCH CREATION
│   ├── Write all 5-7 posts using templates
│   ├── Don't edit while writing (creation mode only)
│   ├── Use timer: 12-15 min per post max
│   └── Done > perfect
│
├── 30 min: EDIT & HOOKS
│   ├── Edit each post (separate mode from creation)
│   ├── Add/refine hooks (use Hook Library)
│   ├── Add CTAs
│   └── Final polish
│
└── 15 min: SCHEDULE
    ├── Schedule all posts for the week
    ├── Set engagement reminders
    └── Done!

DAILY (15 min/day)
├── Check notifications and reply to comments (10 min)
├── Capture 1-2 content ideas in Idea Bank (5 min)
└── Optional: Instagram Stories / casual content

WEEKLY REVIEW (15 min, end of week)
├── Check metrics: What performed best?
├── Flag top 20% for recycling
├── Note what topics/hooks resonated
└── Adjust next week's plan if needed
```

### Phase 4: Recycling Engine

```
RECYCLING RULES

TRACK: Weekly engagement metrics for all posts
FLAG: Top 20% performers (highest engagement rate)
WAIT: 4-6 weeks after original publish date
REWRITE: Same core idea, different angle/template/hook
REPOST: Publish as new content (not a repost)

RECYCLING TRACKER:
| Original Post | Date | Engagement | Recycle Date | New Angle | New Hook |
|---------------|------|------------|--------------|-----------|----------|
| [Title] | [Date] | [Rate] | [Date + 6w] | [Angle] | [Hook] |
```

### Phase 5: Idea Capture System

Set up the always-on idea capture:
- **Phone notes app folder:** "Content Ideas" — dump anything, anytime
- **Screenshot folder:** "Content Inspo" — save others' posts that inspire
- **Voice memos:** Label as "CONTENT:" when capturing spoken ideas
- **Daily prompt:** "What did I learn/notice/feel today that my audience would relate to?"
- **Weekly review:** Process raw ideas → Idea Bank → match to templates
