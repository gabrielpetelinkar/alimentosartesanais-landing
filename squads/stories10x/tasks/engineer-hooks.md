## Action Items

### Phase 1: Build the Hook Library (50+ Hooks)

Generate hooks across 8 categories, tailored to the creator's niche:

**Category 1: Contrarian Hooks (7-8 hooks)**
Pattern: Challenge a commonly held belief
- "Why [common advice] is actually ruining your [result]"
- "I stopped [popular practice] and [unexpected positive result]"
- "The [industry] doesn't want you to know this about [topic]"
- "[Popular method] is dead. Here's what works in [year]"

**Category 2: Question Hooks (7-8 hooks)**
Pattern: Create curiosity through a question they can't ignore
- "What if everything you know about [topic] is wrong?"
- "Can you [desirable outcome] without [thing they think is required]?"
- "Why do 97% of [audience] fail at [goal]?"
- "What would change if you could [dream outcome] in [short time]?"

**Category 3: Number/List Hooks (7-8 hooks)**
Pattern: Specificity creates credibility
- "[N] things I wish I knew before [experience]"
- "[N] mistakes killing your [metric] (and what to do instead)"
- "I studied [N] [successful people] and found [pattern]"
- "[N]-step framework for [desirable outcome]"

**Category 4: Story Hooks (5-6 hooks)**
Pattern: Open a narrative loop
- "I was [vulnerable situation] when [unexpected thing happened]..."
- "3 years ago I almost [dramatic event]. Here's what saved me..."
- "Nobody believed me when I said [bold claim]. Then..."
- "The worst day of my [career/life] taught me [lesson]"

**Category 5: Challenge Hooks (5-6 hooks)**
Pattern: Dare the viewer to engage
- "Try this for 7 days and watch what happens to your [metric]"
- "I bet you can't [simple but effective action] for 30 days"
- "Stop [bad habit] for 1 week. I dare you."

**Category 6: Result Hooks (5-6 hooks)**
Pattern: Lead with proof
- "How I went from [bad state] to [good state] in [timeframe]"
- "[Specific result] in [specific timeframe] — here's the exact process"
- "My [client/student] [achieved result] using this one [method]"

**Category 7: Behind-the-Scenes Hooks (5-6 hooks)**
Pattern: Offer exclusive access
- "Here's what actually happens when [process your audience is curious about]"
- "I'm showing you my exact [system/routine/process] for [result]"
- "The real reason [industry insider knowledge]"

**Category 8: Confession Hooks (5-6 hooks)**
Pattern: Vulnerability creates connection
- "I've been lying to you about [topic]. Here's the truth."
- "The thing about [topic] that nobody talks about..."
- "I was wrong about [previous advice]. Here's what I learned."
- "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement about industry]"

### Phase 2: Hook-Optimize All Content Pieces

For each content piece in the multiplication matrix:

1. **Read the content** — understand the core message
2. **Select 3 hook categories** that fit the message
3. **Write 3-5 hook variations** for each piece
4. **Rank hooks** by predicted stopping power (1-5)
5. **Select the primary hook** (highest rank)
6. **Create backup hook** (second highest — for A/B testing)

**Hook Optimization Template:**
```
Content: [Title/description]
Platform: [Where it will be published]
Core Message: [One sentence]

Hook Variation 1: [Hook text] — Category: [type] — Score: [1-5]
Hook Variation 2: [Hook text] — Category: [type] — Score: [1-5]
Hook Variation 3: [Hook text] — Category: [type] — Score: [1-5]

PRIMARY HOOK: [Selected]
A/B TEST HOOK: [Backup]
```

### Phase 3: Platform-Specific Hook Adaptation

**Text Platforms (LinkedIn, X, Newsletter):**
- First 2 lines MUST be the hook (before "see more" / fold)
- Use line breaks for punch
- Bold claims or specific numbers

**Video Platforms (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts):**
- First 1-2 seconds: Visual hook (movement, text overlay, unusual image)
- First 3 seconds: Verbal hook (opening statement)
- Text on screen reinforces verbal hook
- Thumbnail IS a hook (for YouTube)

**Carousel/Slides (Instagram, LinkedIn):**
- Slide 1 = The Hook (nothing else — just the hook)
- Bold text, contrasting colors, minimal words
- Creates enough curiosity to swipe

**Audio (Podcast, Twitter Spaces):**
- First 5 seconds: Tone of voice IS the hook (energy, pause, whisper)
- Opening statement must create tension or curiosity
- "Let me tell you something nobody in [industry] will admit..."

### Phase 4: Hook Testing Plan

```
HOOK TESTING PROTOCOL

Week 1: Test 5 hook categories across 10 pieces
- Track: 3-second retention (video), engagement rate (text)
- Metric: Which CATEGORY performs best for this audience?

Week 2: Double down on winning categories
- Create 10 variations of the top 2 categories
- Track: Same metrics
- Metric: Which SPECIFIC HOOKS within winning categories work?

Week 3: Optimize and scale
- Use winning hooks as templates for all future content
- Create "Hook Formulas" from patterns that work
- Add to permanent Hook Library

Metrics to Track:
- Video: 3-sec retention rate, watch-through rate
- Text: Engagement rate (likes + comments / impressions)
- Carousel: Swipe-through rate
- Email: Open rate (subject line = hook)
- All: Save rate (indicates high value)
```

## Veto Conditions
- Hooks are generic/not niche-specific → STOP, tailor to audience's pain points
- Hooks are clickbait (promise what content doesn't deliver) → STOP, rewrite to match content
- Same hook style used for every piece → STOP, vary across categories
- No testing plan → WARN, hooks must be tested to find winners
- Hooks don't match platform norms → STOP, adapt for platform
