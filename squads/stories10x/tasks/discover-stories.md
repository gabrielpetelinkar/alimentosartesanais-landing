## Elicitation (Required)

### Step 1: Story Mining — Origin Stories
1. "What's YOUR origin story? How did you get into this field?" → captures: origin_story
2. "What was the moment you realized this was your path?" → captures: epiphany_moment
3. "What were you doing BEFORE this? What was your 'old life'?" → captures: before_state

### Step 2: Story Mining — Transformation Stories
1. "What's the biggest transformation you've created for someone else?" → captures: client_transformation
2. "What's the biggest transformation YOU went through in this field?" → captures: personal_transformation
3. "What did life look like BEFORE vs AFTER that transformation?" → captures: before_after

### Step 3: Story Mining — Struggle & Lesson Stories
1. "When did you almost give up? What kept you going?" → captures: struggle_story
2. "What's the biggest mistake you made in this field? What did you learn?" → captures: mistake_lesson
3. "What's the counterintuitive lesson you've learned that most people in your field don't know?" → captures: contrarian_insight

### Step 4: Story Mining — Daily & Behind-the-Scenes
1. "What does a typical day look like for you?" → captures: daily_story
2. "What's something about your work that would surprise people?" → captures: surprise_element
3. "What question do you get asked most often? What's your answer?" → captures: faq_story

### Step 5: BrandScript (SB7)
1. "Who is your ideal customer/audience? What do they want?" → captures: character_want
2. "What's the main PROBLEM they face? (external, internal, philosophical)" → captures: problem
3. "What makes YOU the right guide? (empathy + authority)" → captures: guide_credentials
4. "What's your plan? (3-step process to solve their problem)" → captures: plan
5. "What does SUCCESS look like for them?" → captures: success_vision
6. "What does FAILURE look like if they don't act?" → captures: failure_stakes

## Action Items

### Phase 1: Story Mining (5 Mines)

**Mine 1: Origin Stories (2-3 stories)**
Dig into:
- How you got started
- Your "aha moment"
- The path that led you here
- Format: Backstory → Turning Point → New Direction

**Mine 2: Transformation Stories (2-3 stories)**
Dig into:
- Your personal transformation
- Client/student transformations
- Before/after contrast
- Format: Before State → Catalyst → After State

**Mine 3: "I Almost Gave Up" Stories (1-2 stories)**
Dig into:
- Lowest moments
- What kept you going
- How you overcame
- Format: Crisis → Decision → Breakthrough

**Mine 4: Lesson-Learned Stories (2-3 stories)**
Dig into:
- Biggest mistakes
- Counterintuitive insights
- Things you wish you knew earlier
- Format: Situation → Mistake/Discovery → Lesson

**Mine 5: Client Success Stories (2-3 stories)**
Dig into:
- Best client transformations
- Unexpected results
- Testimonial-worthy moments
- Format: Client's Problem → Your Solution → Their Result

### Phase 2: Story Bank Creation

For each story, document:
```
STORY #{number}
- Title: [Descriptive title]
- Type: [Origin | Transformation | Struggle | Lesson | Client Success]
- One-Line Summary: [Single sentence]
- Emotional Core: [The feeling this story evokes]
- Key Message: [The lesson or takeaway]
- Resonance Score: [1-5, based on emotional impact + relatability]
- Use Cases: [Where this story works best: social, email, webinar, sales page, etc.]
- SB7 Alignment: [Which SB7 element it supports]
```

### Phase 3: BrandScript (SB7) Assembly

Using the elicitation answers, build:
```
BRANDSCRIPT
1. CHARACTER: [Who is your customer and what do they want?]
2. PROBLEM:
   - External: [The tangible problem]
   - Internal: [How the problem makes them FEEL]
   - Philosophical: [Why this is WRONG/unfair]
   - Villain: [What/who is causing the problem]
3. GUIDE:
   - Empathy: [Show you understand their struggle]
   - Authority: [Prove you can help — credentials, results, experience]
4. PLAN:
   - Step 1: [First action they take]
   - Step 2: [Next step]
   - Step 3: [Final step to success]
5. CALL TO ACTION:
   - Direct: [The main CTA — buy, sign up, book call]
   - Transitional: [The soft CTA — free resource, newsletter]
6. SUCCESS: [Paint the picture of their transformed life]
7. FAILURE: [Paint the picture of what happens if they don't act]
```

### Phase 4: One-Liner Creation

Format: "[Problem]. [Solution]. [Result]."

Example: "Most artisanal food producers struggle to meet safety regulations without losing their authentic methods. We provide a step-by-step compliance framework designed for small producers. So you can sell with confidence and grow your business legally."

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Minimum 10 stories in the Story Bank
- [ ] Each story has all documented fields (title, type, summary, emotional core, key message, score, use cases, SB7 alignment)
- [ ] BrandScript has all 7 elements completed
- [ ] One-Liner follows Problem → Solution → Result format
- [ ] Stories span at least 3 different types (not all the same)
- [ ] Top 3 stories have resonance score of 4+

## Veto Conditions
- No authentic personal stories found → STOP, need deeper mining or different angle
- Creator cannot articulate their audience's problem → STOP, clarify before proceeding
- All stories are about the creator, none about clients → WARN, need client stories for social proof
- BrandScript character is vague → STOP, specify the exact person
